ABOUT US

Favio can change the way you move things, it let’s you access the city like never before. We’re an app that connects you to the nearest delivery partner who can pick up items, make purchases from any store or restaurants in the city and bring them to you in the most sustainable way.

It’s never easy to drop off packages or make purchases when you get busy with work, get stuck in traffic, or you might even end up forgetting about it completely.

All you need to do is, Tell us where to go, what needs to be done and when. While you can Sit back, and let us worry about your task-at-hand.
